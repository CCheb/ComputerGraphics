Colors in HSL follow the Hue, Saturation, and Lightness format

Hue helps to display the color with 0 being red, 120 being green, and 240 being blue. Colors range from 0 to 360 like a color wheel. Saturation dictates how intense the color should be with 100% saturation showing the brightest/most color while 0% shows none of the color i.e. gray. Finally Lightness dictates how much light should be applied to the color with 50% being neither black nor white, 0% being no light (black), and 100% being full light (white)
